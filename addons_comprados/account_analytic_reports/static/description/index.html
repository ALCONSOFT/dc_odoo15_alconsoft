Generate Financial reports and Balance sheets in Odoo!!

Maintaining financial reports is a must to keep a track of daily sales, profits and losses on your Odoo website. Odoo Financial reports by analytic accounts module enables you to choose the analytic accounts you want to include in the reports.

It facilitates the generation of customized reports. The Odoo user can easily generate Financial reports and Balance sheets reports for any valid time period for only the desired analytic accounts.
